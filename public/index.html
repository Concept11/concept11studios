WHO WE ARE

OUR STORY

PAUL THOMAS

co-founder

As a computer kid since the age of four, a career in web and app development seemed to be a hidden destiny. However, throughout my high school and college years, it didn’t always seem so. I was twenty years old, about to graduate with a Bachelor’s degree in Health and Human Performance. During the last semester of my academics, I discovered a passion that I decided to pursue; web and app development. At first it started as a way to help others with websites for their businesses, and eventually turned into a dream to create digital business solutions that are both functional and pleasing to the eye.



CAYMAN JEFFERS

co-founder

As a young kid I had a burning passion to learn and understand the world around me, I was obsessed with reading and always asking questions. My desire to learn went beyond just regurgitating facts from textbooks and listening to lectures, I wanted to understand things at a deeper level and opted to teach myself through most of school. That self reliance and hunger for knowledge lead to my interest in creating software, a field that requires the constant addition of new skills and the ability to learn new languages, softwares and design principles on the fly as they evolve. As a developer with experience in java, c#, javascript, css, html, design and editing softwares, a multitude of IDE’s, game engines and the ability and desire to learn whatever needed, I’m confident and excited to exceed the expectations of all those we create for. As a kid who grew up in family of entrepreneurs I have seen firsthand the sacrifice and dedication it takes to run a business, and I strive for excellence in not only the software we produce but also the relationships we build, effective and honest communication and the best business practices.


THE PROCESS






PLANNING


Learning Your Business

It is critical in our initial meeting to understand your business and your vision. This will allow for a solution that is aligned with your needs.

Gathering Information

Our first meeting will also be a grounds to gather information from you regarding social media, external links, hosting data, and any other information required to build your product.

Flowchart and Timeline

Once receiving information about your business, we can start to put together a flowchart and timeline of when the solution will be finished and key points that certain aspects of the product will be completed.

Design Portfolio

Following our initial meeting, we will develop an illustrative portfolio of the designs, fonts, colors, and images being used in your product. This will help you get an idea of what the final product will look like.





CREATING


Focus on Your Business

You will have no worries while your project is being completed. We will be working away and completing the tasks defined in our timeline.

Weekly Updates

Every week you will be updated on the progress of your project. An updated flowchart with progress notes and a prototype of your project will be included.

Voice Your Opinion

Let us know what you like and don't like! We will adjust the project to what feels best for you. If you have any questions regarding the development stages or integration of your solution, feel free to ask.





LAUNCHING


Optimization

We will wrap the project up by optimizing performance for devices that it will be ran on. This will include social media optimization, responsive design, bug fixing, and testing.

Polishing

By revisiting the almost complete version of the project, we will be able to discuss any last-minute changes. Want to change up the colors and fonts? No problem.

Integration

The part you've been waiting for! Your project will be integrated into your current business setup. All hosting will be handled so you can just visit your project from anywhere! Your finalized source code will be included in this step.

Follow-up

You will be able to contact us about any issues that you run into after your project is released and running. We want to ensure that your project is completely meeting your needs.
